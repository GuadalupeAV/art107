Facial Recognition

Zenitsu Cube

For my facial recognition, I decided to use a gif showing Zenitsu, a character from Demon Slayer. I choose one of my favorite scenes where he appears in for the cube since the cube is shown spinning, almost demonstrating the crazy and emotional persona he potrays. This character jumps from one emotion to another so I figured he was perfect for the spinning cube. This assignment was simple but very fun to explore with. I would like to try this gif with a sphere rather than a cube at a later time.
